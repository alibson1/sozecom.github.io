Well come to SOZECOM Nigeria

Historically, the idea of establishing Zakat and Sadaqa Committee as an institution under modern administration in Sokoto State was started by former military Governor of Sokoto State, Colonel Yakubu Muazu in conjunction with the Sultanate Council in 1997 in order to bridge the gap between reach and poor.

Read More
Our Vision

To establish a solid and reliable system of collection and management of Zakat and Endowment for the benefit of the needy in the society.

Our Mission

To become a formidable Zakat and Endowment Institution, that could cater for the less privileged in Sokoto State and the country in general.

Our Objectives
  1. Overall collection, management and distribution of Zakat and Endowment.
  2. Charitable (Fisabilillah) Programmes in the areas of assisting the needy (e.g. settling of medical and other health services bills).
  3. Rehabilitating psychiatric patients and assisting victims of disaster
  4. Provision of shelter and settling of qualified debts.
  5. Provision of food assistance to the needy on special occasions.
  6. Educating of new Muslims reverts.
  7. Provision of welfare packages and training of orphans and the disabled.
  8. Dawah and public enlightenment activities on the significance of Zakat and Endowment in Islam.
  9. Investment in Halal businesses for income generation among others.
